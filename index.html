Gamification Leaderboard

For class of Jan-2018

  • This is an open-source project classroom-gamification
  • This leaderboard is developed and maintained by Kulbhushan Chand
  • For viewing raw data, students can send your query to Dr. Arun Khosla (course instructor)

The rules are simple -

1) Base score of 50 points is given to all at start.
2) +5 points for every lecture attended.
3) -5 points for each lecture missed.
4) +15 points for each surprise question answered. (We will randomly call a name)
5) -10 points for each time you were absent when your name was called for surprise question. (We will randomly call a name)
6) +25 points each for the best idea given in the class. (You can voluntarily give your idea)
7) +10 points each time for winning lottery. (We will randomly call you)

-- Maximum of 2 best ideas will be accepted each day.
-- Maximum of 2 surprise question each day.
-- Maximum of 2 lottery lucky winners each day.
